## ⚡ 5-Minute Setup

### Step 1: Go to Settings (30 seconds)
1. Open: https://github.com/Souravjr0/Souravjr0
2. Click **Settings** tab (top-right)

### Step 2: Enable Pages (1 minute)
1. Click **Pages** in left sidebar
2. Under "Source":
   - Select Branch: **`main`** (or default branch)
   - Select Folder: **`/ (root)`**
3. Click **Save**

### Step 3: Wait for Deployment (2 minutes)
- GitHub will deploy automatically
- You'll see a green checkmark when ready

### Step 4: Visit Your Site (30 seconds)
- Go to: **https://souravjr0.github.io**
- Your beautiful portfolio is now LIVE! 🎉
