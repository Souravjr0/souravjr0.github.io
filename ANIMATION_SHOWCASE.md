## 🎨 Key Animation Techniques Used

### **1. Scroll-Triggered Animations**
- When you scroll to a section, it animates in
- Uses Intersection Observer (efficient, smooth)
- Creates sense of "discovery" as you scroll

### **2. Staggered Animations**
- Multiple elements animate with slight delays between them
- Creates flowing, choreographed feel
- More professional than all-at-once

### **3. Parallax Effect**
- Background moves at different speed than foreground
- Subtle effect as you scroll
- Adds depth and sophistication

### **4. Hover Effects**
- Elements respond to mouse movement
- Gives feedback that they're interactive
- Encourages exploration

### **5. Smooth Transitions**
- All changes use cubic-bezier timing
- No jarring movements
- Professional, polished feel

### **6. Counter Animations**
- Numbers increment smoothly
- Draws attention to quantitative achievements
- Psychology: numbers are powerful
